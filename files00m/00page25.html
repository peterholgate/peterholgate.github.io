Key Lime Pie


Ingredients

Crust:

1 x 250g Crackers/Biscuits

1 x Cup Mixed Nuts, Walnuts, Pecans, Almonds, Cashews etc.

½ x Cup Sweetened Flaked Coconut

1 x 120g Unsalted Butter melted

1 x Pinch of Salt

Filling:

1 x 400g Sweetened Condensed Milk

3 x Limes, zest only

1 x 18g Fresh squeezed Lime Juice (Key limes would be good)

1 x Tablespoon 100% Pure Vanilla Extract

6 x Large Eggs Yolks

1 x Pinch of Salt

Whipped Cream:

2 x Cups Heavy Whipping Cream

4  x Tablespoons Powdered Sugar

1 x Teaspoon 100% Pure Vanilla Extra


Method

1) Preheat Oven to 162°C and have a 25cm ovenproof Pie Pan ready

2) To prepare the crust process all ingredients until you reach the consistency of wet sand

3) Pack the crust in the pie pan ensuring thick sides and a thin bottom.

4) Pre-Bake for 10 minutes, take out of the oven and cool at least 15/20 minutes before filling.

5) Reduce Oven Temperature to 150°C.

6) Mix the condensed milk, lime zest, vanilla and egg yolks mix well and add the lime juice and quickly incorporate.

7) Pour into the Pre-Bake shell and bake for 30 minutes, or until the filling has set and does not “jiggle” on the middle.

8) Let the pie rest for about 60 minutes at room temp and refrigerate for at least 3 to 4 hours (better overnight).

9) Pipe on the whipped cream in swirls around the edge only.

10) Sprinkle with more Lime zest and finally dust with icing sugar.

Tip 1: For the Whipped Cream: Cool the bowl in the freezer for at least 30 minutes before piping.
