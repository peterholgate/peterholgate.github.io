Caper Sauce for Meat


INGREDIENTS

2 x Teaspoons of capers

1 x Clove of garlic crushed finely

1 x Teaspoon of fresh lemon zest

4 x Tablespoons of butter

1 x Teaspoon of fresh lemon juice

½ x Teaspoon of parsley


METHOD

1) In a small pan, heat butter; add garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice and capers.

2) Stirring continuously, simmer over low heat for 30 seconds.

3) Add the parsley.

4) Remove from heat and serve.

CurryWurst Sauce

Serves 6


INGREDIENTS

2 x Tablespoons Ghee or Olive Oil

1 x Finely chopped large yellow onion.

1 x Tablespoon curry powder

1 x Tablespoon. hot paprika

1 x Can 400g chopped tomatoes

1 x Half cup sugar

1 x Quarter cup red wine vinegar

Salt to taste


METHOD

1) Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.

2) Add 1 finely chopped large yellow onion; cook until soft, about 10 minutes.

3) Add 1 tablespoon of curry powder, 1 tablespoon of hot paprika and cook for 1 minute more.

4) Further crush the canned tomatoes (with juice) into a pan.

5) Add half cup sugar, 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, and salt to taste and stir well.

6) Increase heat to high; bring to a boil.

7) Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.

Caper Sauce with Mustard for Meat


INGREDIENTS

¼ x Cup of capers

1 x Cup of mayonnaise

¼ Cup of sour cream

1 x Tablespoon Dijon mustard

1 x Tablespoon horseradish

1 x Tablespoon white vinegar

½ Teaspoon of brown sugar


METHOD

1) In a large bowl, mix capers, mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, horseradish, vinegar and sugar.

2) Serve.
