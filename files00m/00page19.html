Ingredients


2-3 Teaspoons of Olive Oil

1 x Black pepper to taste

1 x Large leek, finely sliced

1 x 120g Spicy chorizo, skinned and sliced

1 x Teaspoon Turmeric

¼ Teaspoon Hot Smoked Paprika (use double if you like it hot!)

1 x 200g Long grain rice

1 x 800ml Chicken Stock (2 Knorr Cubes)

1 x 200g Frozen Petit Pois (or Peas)

1 x 400g Frozen Seafood mix, defrosted

1 x Lemon cut into 8 wedges



Method


1) Cook the leeks and black pepper in the olive oil for about 5 minutes to soften without browning.

2) Add the chorizo and fry until it releases its oils.

3) Add the turmeric and rice, then stir until coated with oil.

4) Add the chicken stock, bring to boil and simmer for 15 minutes.

5) Add the peas and cook for 5 minutes.

6) Add the seafood and stir in for 2-3 minutes or until the rice is cooked.

7) Serve immediately with the lemon wedges.


Tip 1: Substitute fish stock for chicken stock.

Tip 2: Substitute cubed chicken for seafood or part thereof.

Tip 3: Serve with Fried Eggs, sunny side up cooked in butter.

Easy Paella - Serves 4




Ingredients


2 x Medium lemons

125ml x Water

2 x Tablespoons brown sugar (or honey)

2 x Teaspoons cornflour

1 x Tablespoon sweet chilli sauce

1 x Tablespoon light soy sauce (optional)


Method


1) Using a vegetable peeler, peel off the rind of the lemons, avoiding the white pith. Slice very finely.

2) Cut the lemons in half, juice into a medium pan and add the lemon rind, water and sugar.

3) Bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook till the rind has softened, about 10 minutes.

5) Combine flour with a teaspoon or two of water to make a slurry.

6) Add the sweet chilli and soy sauces to the lemon sauce and whisk in while drizzling in the cornflour slurry, making sure there are no lumps.

7) Simmer for about 5 minutes or until thickened.


Serve with Battered (tempura is best) Chicken or Fish

Chinese Lemon Sauce – Serves 4 - Provisional




Ingredients


1/2 x Small red onion peeled and finely sliced

1 x Clove of Garlic chopped

2 x Tablespoon olive oil

1 x Cup drained and squeezed chopped defrosted frozen spinach or…

…about 450g (16oz) of fresh spinach

4 x Large eggs

1/2 x Cup of pannacotta (or crumbled feta cheese or grated Cheddar Cheese)

Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Dash of Chilli Sauce (hot tabasco type) or more to taste


Method


1) Preheat a 20cm (8in) frying with enough oil in the pan to very lightly coat the bottom.

2) Add the onion and cook until just starting to brown.

3) Add the spinach and garlic and toss for a 2 to 3 mins to heat through, then remove from the heat and allow the vegetables to cool slightly.

3) Beat the eggs in a large bowl.

4) Add in the cooled spinach and red onion, followed by the feta (or other cheese), a good pinch of pepper and a dash of chilli sauce.

5) Pour the mixture into a deep oven dish.

6) Cook in a preheated oven at 160 C for about 20 mins or until nearly set (shake the dish to check).

7) Turn up the heat to 180 C and cook for a further 5 mins or until lightly brown on top.


Serve hot or cold.


Tip 1: Substitute an equal amount of other cooked vegetables for the spinach.


Tip 2: Really good with roasted mixed veg.
