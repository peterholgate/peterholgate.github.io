Sazon Goya Substitute


INGREDIENTS

3 x Tablespoons annatto ground

2 x Tablespoons garlic powder

2 x Tablespoons cumin

2 x Tablespoons coriander powder

1 x Tablespoon oregano ground

¼ x Teaspoon turmeric powder


METHOD

1) Finely grind any coarse ingredients into a powder.

2) Whisk ingredients in a large bowl to mix.

3) Use a funnel to transfer the sazon seasoning into an airtight container.


Tip1 Keeps for a few months
