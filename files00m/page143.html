RECIPE

Chicken and Chorizo with Orzo

Serves 4



INGREDIENTS

4 x Chicken breasts chopped into chunks

200g x Chorizo sliced

2 x Red onions diced

8 x Garlic cloves finely sliced (or use paste)

2 x Roasted red peppers finely sliced

2 x Tablespoons sundried tomato pesto

2 x Tablespoons tomato puree

2 x Tin chopped tomatoes

1 litre x Chicken stock

4 x Teaspoons smoked paprika

1 x Teaspoon chilli flakes

Salt to taste

Black pepper to taste

320g x Orzo pasta

4 x Tablespoons cream cheese

2 x Handfuls spinach

Grated parmesan to taste



METHOD

1) Add the chicken, chorizo, roasted red pepper, red onion, garlic, tinned tomatoes, tomato puree, red pesto, stock, paprika, chilli flakes, salt and pepper into your slow cooker.

2) Mix well and cook on low for 4 hours or high for 2 hours.

3) Stir in the orzo and spinach.

4) Cover and cook for a further 50 mins, stirring halfway so it doesn’t stick.

5) Stir in the cream cheese.

6) Serve topped with grated parmesan.
