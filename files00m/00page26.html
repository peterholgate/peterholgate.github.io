Chinese Lemon Sauce – Serves 4


Ingredients

2 x Medium lemons

125ml x Water

2 x Tablespoons brown sugar (or honey)

2 x Teaspoons cornflour

1 x Tablespoon sweet chilli sauce

1 x Tablespoon light soy sauce (optional)


Method

1) Using a vegetable peeler, peel off the rind of the lemons, avoiding the white pith. Slice very finely.

2) Cut the lemons in half, juice into a medium pan and add the lemon rind, water and sugar.

3) Bring to the boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook till the rind has softened, about 10 minutes.

5) Combine flour with a teaspoon or two of water to make a slurry.

6) Add the sweet chilli and soy sauces to the lemon sauce and whisk in while drizzling in the cornflour slurry, making sure there are no lumps.

7) Simmer for about 5 minutes or until thickened.

Tip1: Serve with Battered (tempura is best) Chicken or Fish
