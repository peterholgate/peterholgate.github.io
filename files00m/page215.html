Traditional Beef Stroganoff

Serves 2


INGREDIENTS

1 x 400 g Sirloin steak cut against the grain into thin strips

2 x Tablespoon plain flour

1 x 40g Butter

1 x 200g Button mushrooms halved

2 x Small brown onion thinly sliced

4 x Tablespoon brandy

1 x Teaspoon paprika

1 x Teaspoon cinnamon

1 x Stock cube + 4 Tablespoon of water

1 x 140g creme fraiche

2 x Tablespoon lemon juice


INSTRUCTIONS

1) Place your beef strips in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.

2) Add 1 Tablespoon of plain flour and mix to coat.

3) In a large frying pan, melt the butter.

4) Add onions and fry for 2 minutes on medium heat.

5) Add mushrooms and carry on cooking for another 3 minutes, until both the onions and mushrooms have softened.

6) Gather the mushrooms and onions to one side of the pan. Increase the heat to high and tip in the beef to the other side of the frying pan. Stirring, flash-fry the beef for 1-2 minutes until just browned all over.

7) Add the brandy and let most of it evaporate, stirring the beef, mushrooms and onions together (this shouldn't take longer than 20 seconds). Crumble in half a stock cube.

8) Add paprika and cinnamon.

9) Add 4 Tablespoon of water in.

10) Add creme fraiche and mix together. Only cook until it's warmed through, then take off the heat before the beef has time to overcook.

11) Just before serving, stir in a Tablespoon of lemon juice.


Tip1 Good Served with French fries and boiled broccoli.

Stuffed Peppers Italian

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

4 x Medium to large red bell peppers (or green)

1 x 700g lean ground beef

1 x Medium finely chopped onion

1 x 250g Cooked rice

2 x Teaspoons Italian seasoning

½ x Teaspoon Garlic Powder

1/2 x Teaspoon salt

1/4 x Teaspoon Black Pepper (Ground)

2 x 400g Cans tomato sauce (or blend chopped tomatoes)

1/2 x Cup shredded mozzarella cheese


DIRECTIONS

1) Preheat oven to 200C.

2) Cut off tops of bell peppers, remove seeds and if necessary, cut thin slice from bottom of each pepper so they stand upright.

3) Brown ground beef and onion in large skillet on medium-high heat.

4) Drain the fat, then, add the rice, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper and mix until well blended.

5) Pour 1 can of the tomato sauce into bottom of 20cm square baking dish.

6) Stuff peppers with beef mixture.

7) Place stuffed peppers upright in baking dish.

8) Pour remaining can tomato sauce over stuffed peppers and cover with foil.

9) Bake 35 to 45 minutes or until peppers are tender.

10) Remove foil and sprinkle stuffed peppers with cheese.

11) Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.

12) Cut stuffed peppers in half and spoon sauce in dish over each half to serve.

13) Serve with crusty bread or similar.

Chicken and Aubergine Parmigiana

Serves: 4


INGREDIENTS

4 x Tablespoons of Olive Oil

1 x Medium eggplant (350g), cut lengthways in 1cm thick slices

1 x 600g chicken breast fillets, cut lengthways in 1cm-thick slices

2 x Tablespoons lemon juice

2 x Teaspoons dried oregano

1 x 100ml Tomato puree (or 1 cup of passata)

1 x 400g Can chopped tomatoes

1 x Tablespoon balsamic vinegar

1/2 x Cup mature cheddar cheese, grated

1/2 Cup grated parmesan cheese

¼ x Cup panko breadcrumbs

2 x Cloves garlic, crushed

1 x Extra fresh oregano (optional)

 1 x Garlic bread (optional see below)


INSTRUCTIONS

1 ) Preheat oven to 180°C.

2) Coat a 2-litre (30x 22cm) ovenproof dish with oil.

3) Microwave or steam eggplant slices for 2 minutes or until tender and set aside.

4) Cover a large frying pan with oil and cook chicken over a high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden.

5) Add lemon juice and sprinkle with dried oregano.

6) Place chicken in dish.

7) Add eggplant slices interspersed with chicken pieces.

8) Combine tomato pasta sauce with tomatoes, garlic and balsamic then spoon over chicken and eggplant.

9) Sprinkle with cheese and breadcrumbs.

10) Bake for 30 minutes.

11) Sprinkle with fresh oregano before serving.

Garlic Bread:

12) Coat each piece of bread with olive oil then grill on a hot plate to get char marks or place under a hot grill to toast.

13) Combine garlic and fresh herbs parsley or oregano in a small bowl with olive oil.

14) Top each bread slice with fresh herb and garlic mixture.

15) Serve with the above.


Tip 1: Serve sprinkled with 4 cups green salad with 4 tablespoons vinaigrette (2 parts olive oil to 1 part balsamic vinegar)
