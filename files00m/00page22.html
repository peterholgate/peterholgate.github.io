Pork with Lentils - Serves 8


Ingredients


1.2kg  x Lean pork cut into 3 x 3 cm  cubes

1 x 300g Red lentils (split), washed and drained

4 x Onion medium, Spanish sliced coarsely

4 x Carrots medium, sliced into large chunks

12 x Tablespoon of melted beef dripping (or olive oil)

1 x Teaspoon ground black pepper

1/8 x Teaspoon cayenne pepper

1½ x Teaspoon Herbes de Provence to taste.

2 x Bay leaves (Med)

4 x Cloves garlic, rough chopped

1.25 x Litres of chicken stock or use cubes

1.25 x Litres of boiling water (if not using chicken stock)

 1 x Tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped


Method


1) Add chicken stock cubes to boiling water and mix well or use stock.

2) Pour dripping or oil into large heavy pot on the hob.

3) Add the pork, cayenne and half of the black pepper.

4) Cook on a hob at high heat until outside is seared light brown.

5) Add the onion.

6) Cook at medium heat until the onion is transparent.

7) Add carrots and cook for 2 min.

8) Add chicken stock and and washed lentils, mix.

9) Place bay leaves on top and put heavy lid on.

10) Transfer to an oven and cook for 90 mins at 180C.

11) Test at 90 mins lentils and pork must be soft and well cooked.

12) Cook for a further 30 mins if necessary.

13) Mix well and sprinkle on the remaining black pepper.

14) Sprinkle the Herbes de Provence on top.

15) Remove lid and cook at 200C for a further 5 mins.

16) Garnish with chopped parsley and serve.


Tip 1 Add 2 x rough chopped green peppers (Capsicums) as a variation

Tip 2 Good served with new potatoes in butter and garnished with parsley

Tip 3 Use a heavy pot that you can also use in an oven
