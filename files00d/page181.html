Aubergine and Spinach

Serves 6


INGREDIENTS

3 x Aubergines (1kg), sliced thinly lengthways

2 to 3 x Teaspoons of fine salt for salting the Aubergine etc

8 x Tablespoons olive oil

1 x 200g Mascarpone cheese

Cherry Tomatoes, chopped in 2

1 x 200g Mozzarella cheese sliced

1 x 100g Grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese

3 x Cloves of garlic, peeled and chopped

1 x Freshly ground black pepper to taste

1 x 200g Tomato purée (or blended canned tomatoes)

1 x Bunch of fresh basil (can use dried)


METHOD

1) Thinly layer the aubergine slices on a large oven dish then spray with olive oil.

2) Sprinkle lightly with salt.

3) Bake in the oven for 25 minutes at 200C.

4) Chop the spinach.

5) Add the mascarpone cheese (or use ricotta cheese)

6) Add basil, black pepper and salt.

7) Transfer the aubergines to a large, deep circular dish overlapping the edges.

8) Add a layer of aubergines to the bottom of the dish.

9) Add a layer of mascarpone and spinach mix.

10 )Add a spaced layer of tomatoes and mozzarella slices.

11) Add a layer of tomato paste.

12)Add a layer of aubergine slices.

13)Add another layer of mascarpone and spinach mix.

14) Add another spaced layer of tomatoes and mozzarella slices.

15)Add another layer of tomato paste.

16) Add a final layer of aubergines.

17) Fold the overlapped aubergine edge to cover the aubergine slices and form a top.

18) Cover the aubergine top with parmesan.

19) Sprinkle with black pepper.

20) Bake in the oven for 30 minutes 180C.


Tip1 Serve with parmesan, fresh basil and black pepper.


Tip2: Prepare a day early so that all you have to do is cook for 30mins on the day.

Frittata with Spinach

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

1/2 x Small red onion peeled and finely sliced

1 x Clove of Garlic chopped

2 x Tablespoon olive oil

1 x Cup drained and squeezed chopped defrosted frozen spinach or…

…about 450g (16oz) of fresh spinach

4 x Large eggs

1/2 x Cup of pannacotta, crumbled feta or grated Cheddar Cheese

1 x Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

1 x Dash of Chilli Sauce (hot tabasco type) or more to taste


METHOD

1) Preheat a 20cm (8in) frying with enough oil in the pan to very lightly coat the bottom.

2) Add the onion and cook until just starting to brown.

3) Add the spinach and garlic and toss for a 2 to 3 mins to heat through, then remove from the heat and allow the vegetables to cool slightly.

3) Beat the eggs in a large bowl.

4) Add in the cooled spinach and red onion, followed by the feta (or other cheese), a good pinch of pepper and a dash of chilli sauce.

5) Pour the mixture into a deep oven dish.

6) Cook in a preheated oven at 160 C for about 20 mins or until nearly set (shake the dish to check).

7) Turn up the heat to 180 C and cook for a further 5 mins or until lightly brown on top.

8) Serve hot or cold.


Tip 1: Substitute an equal amount of other cooked vegetables for the spinach.

Tip 2: Really good with roasted mixed veg.

Sprouts Xmas

Serving 4-6


INGREDIENTS

1kg x Brussels sprouts

25g x Butter

100g x Cooking chorizo, finely diced

100g x Chestnuts (tinned or vacuum packed)

1 x Thyme sprigs

1 x Salt, ground black pepper, to taste


METHOD

1) Preheat the oven to 200C/Gas Mark 6.

2) Rinse the sprouts, trim the ends, discard any damaged or dry outer leaves, then cut into 5mm slices.

3) Place on a baking tray and dot with butter, season with salt and pepper then roast for 15 minutes.

4) Remove the sprouts and add the chorizo, chestnuts and thyme and cook for a further 10-15 minutes.

5) The sprouts should be a rich golden colour and soft when pierced, and the chorizo should be piping hot and just beginning to char at the edges.
