Spicy Tomato Chicken (Jamie Oliver)


INGREDIENTS

2 x Chicken thighs, skin on
2 x Chicken drumsticks, skin on
3 x Tablespoons olive oil
1 x 400g Can of chopped tomatoes
1 x Half onion, medium chopped
2 x Garlic cloves. Finely chopped
1 x Teaspoon chilli flakes
1 x Teaspoon thyme
1 x Teaspoon oregano
1 x Pinch of sugar
1 x Salt and black pepper to taste


METHOD

1) First make the sauce.

2) Cook the garlic and onion with the thyme in 2 tablespoons of olive oil over a gentle heat until the onion is soft and transparent.

3) Pour in the chopped tomatoes, the chilli flakes, oregano and sugar.

4) Season with salt and pepper and simmer for 20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
5) Preheat oven to 180C.

6) Pour 1 tablespoon of olive oil into a frying or saute pan, add the chicken pieces and heat over a moderate/high heat to brown the chicken pieces all over.
7) Place the browned chicken pieces into a shallow ovenproof dish and pour over the sauce.

8) Cover and cook in the oven for 40 minutes. Serve.

Chicken Supreme

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

1 x 50ml Extra virgin olive oil
2 x Small onion, finely chopped
2 x Garlic clove, finely chopped
1 x Teaspoon dried coriander
2 x Heaped teaspoon dried parsley
1 x Lemon 
2 x Medium sized tub of single cream
4 x Chicken breasts, diced
1 x salt and pepper to taste
1 x Tagliatelle pasta

METHOD
1) Pan fry the chicken in a pan with the dried coriander, then leave to one side.
2) Put the pasta on, then start making your sauce by adding a small glug of the olive oil then put in the onions and the garlic and let the onions soften for about 10 minutes.

3) Add the parsley then the cream (or Bechamel), squeeze the lemon in the sauce then bring to a boil add the chicken, season with salt and pepper, stir.

4) Serve straight away with the pasta.

Blue Cheese Chicken LCHF

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

500g x Chicken breasts

2 x Tablespoons olive oil

½ x Glass white wine

250g x Crumbled blue cheese (2/3 cup)

200ml x Double cream

2 x Cloves garlic

¼ x Teaspoon black pepper

1 x Tablespoon finely chopped Italian parsley


PREPARATION

1) Preheat oven to 190 deg C

2) Slice each chicken breast lengthwise through the middle, creating two thin chicken breasts from the original breast.

3) In an ovenproof saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sear the chicken, cooking three minutes on each side.

4) Add the garlic and black pepper to coat the chicken and fryfor about 1 minute.

5) Add the wine and turn the heat to low, simmering for 8-10 minutes until wine has reduced by half.

6) Add cheese, cream and parsley. Simmer another minute.

7) Put the pan in the oven for 10-15 minutes. Sauce will continue to reduce and become thicker.
