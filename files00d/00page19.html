Recipe PAE-001

Easy Paella - Serves 4


Ingredients

2-3 Teaspoons of Olive Oil

1 x Black pepper to taste

1 x Large leek, finely sliced

1 x 120g Spicy chorizo, skinned and sliced

1 x Teaspoon Turmeric

¼ Teaspoon Hot Smoked Paprika (use double if you like it hot!)

1 x 200g Long grain rice

1 x 800ml Chicken Stock (2 Knorr Cubes)

1 x 200g Frozen Petit Pois (or Peas)

1 x 400g Frozen Seafood mix, defrosted

1 x Lemon cut into 8 wedges



Method

1) Cook the leeks and black pepper in the olive oil for about 5 minutes to soften without browning.

2) Add the chorizo and fry until it releases its oils.

3) Add the turmeric and rice, then stir until coated with oil.

4) Add the chicken stock, bring to boil and simmer for 15 minutes.

5) Add the peas and cook for 5 minutes.

6) Add the seafood and stir in for 2-3 minutes or until the rice is cooked.

7) Serve immediately with the lemon wedges.


Tip 1: Substitute fish stock for chicken stock.

Tip 2: Substitute cubed chicken for seafood or part thereof.

Tip 3: Serve with Fried Eggs, sunny side up cooked in butter.
