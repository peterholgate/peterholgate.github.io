Chicken Chasseur Slow Cooker LCHF

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

6 x Chicken thighs with skin on

1 x Butter, large knob for frying

2 x Onions medium, thinly sliced

3 x Tablespoons of coconut flour (optional)

3 x Garlic cloves, crushed

200g x Mushrooms

400g x Can tomatoes

400ml x Chicken stock

100ml x Wine (white)

1 x Bouquet garni

3 x Bay leaves

1 x Black pepper to taste

1 x Cayenne pepper to taste

1 x 100ml Double cream

1 x Parsley, chopped to taste


METHOD

1) Turn on slow cooker.

2) In a large frying pan sear the chicken in butter until brown.

3) Add chicken to slow cooker

4) Fry the onions until transparent.

5) Add coconut flour and mix well. (optional).

6) Add the garlic and mushrooms then fry, stirring for about 3 mins.

7) Add the stock, tomatoes and the wine.

8) Add the bouquet garni and 3 bay leaves.

9) Bring to the boil then add to the slow cooker.

10) Cook for about 6 hours (use your cooker settings).

11) Pour on the double cream, sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.

Chicken Espagnole for Slow Cookers LCHF

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

1 x 100g Knob butter

4 x Chicken joints

1 x 100g Chorizo, halved and sliced (or substitute smoked bacon)

2 x Medium onions, finely sliced

2 x Peppers (capsicums), chopped into 2cm squares

1 x 150g Button mushrooms, whole

1 x 500ml Chicken mtock, hot

1 x Large carrot, diced small

1 x 150g Tomato puree

2 x Medium bay leaves

1 x Bouquet Garni (or rosemary, rhyme and sage sprigs)

1 x Black pepper to taste

1 x Small glass of sherry


METHOD

1) Brown the chicken joints in a large pan with butter then add to slow cooker.

2) Add the chorizo and fry until it darkens then add to slow cooker.

3) Add the onions and fry to soften about 5 minutes.

4) Add the peppers and fry to soften about 5 minutes.

5) Add the carrot and fry for about 2 minutes.

6) Add the chicken stock and bring to boil.

7) Add the tomato puree, bouquet garni, bay leaves and mushrooms.

8) Season with black pepper, bring to boil, then add to slow cooker.

9) Cook for 6-8 hours on a low setting (or less time on high).

10) Add the sherry about 15 minutes before serving.


Tip1 Add a Teaspoon of Hot smoked paprika.

Tip2 Add a teaspoon og turmeric.

Easy Paella

Serves 4


INGREDIENTS

2 to 3 Tablespoons of olive oil

50g x Knob of butter (optional)

1 x Black pepper to taste

1 x Medium leek, finely sliced

½ x Red pepper, finely chopped

120g x Spicy chorizo, skinned, halved and sliced

1 x Teaspoon turmeric

1 x Teaspoon Hot Smoked Paprika (or hot, smoked paprika)

¼ x Teaspoon of Cayenne Pepper (omit if using hot, smoked paprika)

250g x Long grain rice (Basmati is good)

1 x 900ml Chicken Stock (or use 2 ‘Knorr’ cubes)

200g x Frozen petit pois (or peas)

400g x Seafood mix, defrost, if frozen (or your choice of fish, chicken etc)

1 x Lemon cut into 8 wedges



METHOD


1) Cook the leeks and black pepper in the olive oil and butter for about 5 minutes to soften without browning.

2) Add the chorizo and fry until it releases its oils.

3) Add the red pepper and fry for about 3 mins.

3) Add the turmeric, smoked paprika, cayenne and rice, then stir until coated with oil.

4) Add the chicken stock, bring to boil and simmer for 15 minutes.

5) Add the peas and cook for 5 minutes.

6) Add the seafood and stir in for 2-3 minutes or until the rice is cooked.

7) Serve immediately with the lemon wedges.


Tip 1: Substitute fish stock for chicken stock.

Tip 2: Substitute cubed chicken for seafood or part thereof.

Tip 3: Serve with Fried Eggs, sunny side up cooked in butter.
